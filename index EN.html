NATIONAL INSTITUTE OF APOPA

"Towards Development and Progress Through Education"

History

The National Institute of Apopa was founded on February 7, 1981, at the Vicente Acosta School, starting with a single academic high school section and 44 students. Under the direction of Professor José Jerónimo Yánez and with a small team of teachers and administrative staff, the institution began its growth. In 1984, it acquired its own land, which allowed for the gradual expansion of facilities and the educational offer, including commercial and health high schools in 1988.

Over the years, the institute has stood out for its prestige and national recognition, with comprehensive attention to students in academic, artistic, sports, and cultural areas. Today, it has 68 teachers, 26 administrative staff, and serves 1,681 students, offering various modalities of technical and general high schools. Since 2015, the institute has developed a Renewed Curriculum: Offering Vocational Technical High School in Administrative Accounting, Primary Health Care, Electronics, Software Development, and General High School.

High Schools

The Technical Vocational High School in Accounting Administration aims to train professionals with solid skills and knowledge in the financial and accounting management of companies and institutions. This high school prepares students in areas such as accounting, finance, administration, and auditing, enabling them to perform key tasks in planning and controlling an organization’s economic resources.

Upon completing their studies, students will be equipped to carry out accounting records, conduct financial analyses, manage accounting software, and participate in economic decision-making. In addition, they will have the opportunity to continue their higher education in related fields, such as public accounting, business administration, or economics.

The Technical High School in Electronics graduates as a person with solid technical and human training that allows them to integrate into the workforce, be a productive agent of the country, and promote their personal development.

They can work by providing electronic maintenance to all types of generic circuits that do not involve applications from specific sectors; additionally, they can enter the industry by supporting the implementation of automated processes.

The Technician in Primary Health Care is a medium-level Technician prepared to engage in health promotion processes, community organization, health administration activities, epidemiological surveillance, first aid in emergency situations, disaster prevention, and activities of their profession that allow them to operate autonomously.

This High School graduate can work in basic health care settings, including hospitals, outpatient facilities, and home care, supporting health services.

They acquire general knowledge in all scientific subjects, such as Mathematics, Natural Sciences, Social Sciences, Language, and Literature, among others.

General High School students graduate in two years and are prepared to attend university in various fields. They also gain basic skills to perform effectively in the workforce and business, especially if their goal is to later study any university career or meet immediate job expectations in the sectors of commerce, industry, or as micro-entrepreneurs according to their aspirations.
